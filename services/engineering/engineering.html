Energy Audits

Energy audits are a powerful tool to analyze the energy flows within a facility, and are carried out as a step to improve energy consumption. It is therefore necessary to adopt energy improvement measures for adding a renewable energy system to the facility. We understand the importance of conducting industrial energy audits to lower the increasingly expensive energy costs and help facilities move towards a sustainable future. Based on this, we suggest Energy Conservation Opportunities (ECO) or measures that result in a more efficient use of energy inside the facility, either by partial or global replacement of existing system.

We conduct energy audits in four levels:

Level 0 – Benchmarking: Assessment of the building’s energy cost and efficiency by conducting a brief on-site survey of the building

Level 1 – Walk-through audit: Preliminary analysis is made to assess the energy efficiency, and provide a list of energy conservation measures to be adopted

Level 2 – Detailed energy audit: A Comprehensive study on existing installation in the facility to identify and resolve the defects. This level can involve advanced techniques and computer-based simulation tools to carry out precise evaluation.

Level 3 – Investment-Grade audit: Detailed analysis and engineering study to incorporate modifications in the existing system focusing more on Energy Conservation Measures.

Solar PV Installation & Maintenance

The increasing demand for energy saving solutions in today’s world resulted in the installation of a rooftop solar system to satisfy the power requirements of commercial, residential and industrial facilities. These rooftop solar panels installed on the roofs of buildings capture solar energy that can meet the energy requirements of the company, achieving the benefit of its cost-saving features.

We help you through the process of acquiring the permissions and paperwork to guarantee that your solar panel installation is in compliance with all local and safety regulations. Our team of experts help clients in solar panel installation process by examining different factors in order to maximize its benefits:

Check the roof condition, repair the roof before installation if damaged, check whether adequate space is available, and the availability of sunlight in the roof

Analyze the slope of roof for appropriate rainfall drainage and debris removal

Calculate the amount of weight roof can sustain and provide additional support if required

Assess your buildings energy requirements to select the quantity of solar panels




Like all other systems, solar system also needs regular monitoring and inspections to ensure maximum performance and security. Our service specialists, who are familiar with Photo Voltaic components and safety measures, handle the PV maintenance operations, which include:

Regular and timely cleaning of solar cells and PV panels

Servicing HT side equipment annually

Testing of circuits

Retro-commissioning

Warranty management

Diagnosing reasons of low solar power production

Ensure roof drainage is adequate

Check for ground erosion and corrosion

Measurement & Verification

Measurement & Verification (M&V) include the process of planning, measuring, collecting and analyzing data for the sole purpose of verifying, and reporting actual energy savings generated within a facility resulting from the implementation of energy conservation measures. With the implementation of these services, we are able to draw conclusions on how much energy ECM has avoided using.

We make sincere efforts to verify whether the installed equipment is able to produce expected cost savings. M&V services consist of the following:

Meter installation and maintenance

Gathering and screening of data

Developing a computation method and acceptable estimates

Computations with measured data

Reporting and verification

Electric Vehicles Installation & Maintenance

Electric vehicles have become more popular as companies started to think of the need of more sustainable and environmentally responsible solutions for transportation. The emergence of Electric Vehicles has helped facility managers to provide solutions that are affordable as well as environment-friendly. The rise in fuel prices and the increasing need for greener initiatives have forced many companies to opt for cost-effective solutions that in turn reduced the operating expenses.

With the advent of EV, there has been a significant reduction in greenhouse gas emissions that account for large amounts of air pollution, making it a highly appreciated alternative to gasoline and diesel vehicles. As a facilities service provider, our team accompany the growing demand for charging stations for electric vehicles, their servicing, maintenance, and so on.

Our facility experts analyze the nature and needs of the property, parking spaces, likely demand for electric vehicle charging and marketing benefits while supporting clients in the process. We help organizations to implement renewable energy sources for the charging of electric vehicles in an eco-friendlier way. Electric vehicles require charging which results in a considerable hike in the energy consumption. We help organizations to cut down electricity cost with the installation of renewable energy sources such as solar panels.


The main advantages of Electric Vehicles over conventional vehicles are:

Lower running costs

Low maintenance costs

Zero tailpipe emissions

Tax and financial benefits

Easy and convenient to drive
