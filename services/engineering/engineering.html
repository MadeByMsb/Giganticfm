Our electrical engineering services involve the planning, design, installation, maintenance, and repair of all the electrical systems and equipment inside the building.

Mechanical Systems

As new construction and renovation projects demand minimum energy conservation and increased efficiency, our design teams and engineers are constantly working towards meeting the efficiency expectations of our clients. Our mechanical engineers look upon the design and operation of a facility’s overall mechanical system to achieve the efficiency gain target of the facility.

We help business owners to implement a mechanical system that can be easily maintained and reliably operated at its most efficient points for the life of the equipment, system and building. Our mechanical services for clients include installation and maintenance of boiler, plumbing & piping systems, heating, ventilation & air conditioning systems (HVAC), fire protection & suppression systems, and building automation and control systems.

We design and deliver an efficient mechanical system in the following steps:

Understand the owner’s needs through communication

Determine efficiency design goals

Incorporate an integrated design approach

Perform energy modeling in early design

Include training and O&M manuals in the contract

Lifts, Escalators, and Conveyors

Lifts, escalators, and conveyors are important components in many facilities providing efficient vertical and horizontal transportation of people and goods inside. Proper installation and maintenance of these components can be a time and labour consuming task, and is considered crucial for the safe and reliable operation of the occupants within the facility.

We oversee the installation of lifts and escalators in residential as well as commercial buildings anywhere in UAE. Our team spend a large amount of time conducting routine checks to ensure that the lifts are functioning properly and is safe for use by the occupants. This may involve working with contractors or vendors to install new systems, and coordinating regular maintenance and inspections to ensure that existing systems are functioning properly and meeting safety standards.

In order to reduce the chances of lifts malfunctioning, we further propose a predictive lift maintenance service that receives the lift’s data, does analysis on the collected data to detect anomalies and recommend actions to be taken before downtime.

We’ve got you covered with a wide range of elevator and escalator maintenance services also. With our flexible maintenance services, you can pick from our wide range of services to create a tailored maintenance plan that meets your specific needs rather than settling for a one-size-fits-all solution.

When we choose a maintenance plan for lifts and escalators in your facility, we pay attention on the requirements of your building, people and equipment. Our key considerations are:

Compliance with safety standards

Regular maintenance and inspections

Emergency procedures

Staff training

Documentation and record-keeping

MEP Drafting

We offer MEP drafting services that enable the creation of detailed coordination drawings of various systems before construction thereby enabling a more effective collaboration between various engineering disciplines. We have extensive expertise and experience in providing MEP drawings in appropriate formats as required by the client meeting the industry standards.

Our skilled drafting team work in close contact with the client’s engineering team to ensure the client receives professional drawings with the highest accuracy. Fabrication details and installation locations are carefully embedded in the design to produce accurate drawings.

Aided with the insight and knowledge gained over experience, we are equipped to handle projects of all sizes and complexities. We can produce MEP drawings for a wide range of projects such as high-rise residential towers, shopping malls, medical laboratories, power plants, etc. Apart from 2D drafting using AutoCAD MEP, we have experience in MEP 3D modeling and clash coordination with the help of BIM (Building Information Modelling) software tools.

Our team offer superior quality drafting services with unparalleled precision that no one can match. Our company consists of experienced architects, designers, and draftsmen who can help you deliver your building projects successfully.

Switchgear Assembly

We support our clients with installation and maintenance of switchgear and help them from unexpected financial expenses that may result when the switchgear malfunctions. With our team of experts equipped with the right skills, we are able to implement an effective switchgear testing, inspection, and maintenance program.

We develop a maintenance program that follows both preventive and predictive approaches, including recommendations from the National Fire Protection Association Agency (NFPA) and International Electrical Testing Association (NETA).

Reliability Centered Maintenance (RCM) is another approach we adopt in industries where equipment reliability is critical. After analyzing the system, our experts evaluate the failure modes and determine the most appropriate preventive maintenance activity for each component.


Switchgear Assembly :

Review equipment history

Perform monitoring and visual inspections

Thermal scanning, partial discharge testing etc. to take corrective actions

Identify and keep all materials required for the outage

Develop work plan and schedule

Perform inspections, cleaning and related services during the outage

Prepare a report of test results
