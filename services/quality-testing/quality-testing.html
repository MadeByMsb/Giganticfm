Chemical testing has a significant role in guaranteeing the safety and quality of consumer products while complying with strict regulations at global and regional levels. By conducting chemical analysis, you can minimize safety risks and liabilities, monitor and reduce environmental impact, and you will be more confident in the quality of your products

Our testing laboratories and experienced subject experts are here to help you provide validation that your products comply with global regulations as well as your own specifications. Our solutions are also designed in a way that can assess the impact of chemicals throughout your supply chain.

Our chemical analysis covers testing on a wide range of consumer product categories including food items, toys, textile and footwear, personal care and beauty products, electronic products, packaging materials, pet products, and much more.

Our chemical testing solutions are designed to help you safeguard your products against hazardous substances, hence helping you untangle all the complications with global regulations..

Microbiological Testing

Microorganisms can be dangerous to health if they come in contact with the food we eat and the products we use. Our microbiological testing services evaluate your food and consumer products and detect the presence of harmful pathogens in them.

With microbiological analysis, we help you determine the shelf life of your product, evaluate the environment compatible with its storage, and more. For food and beverage products, this testing can help detect toxic pathogens like Salmonella, E-coli, and other microorganisms that cause food to spoil easily.

We have the equipment, experience, and experts to suggest a customized microbiological testing program based on your specific needs. Our solutions for microbiological testing provide clients with accurate and reliable readings.

Microbiological Testing :

Contamination testing

Shelf-life testing

Stability testing

Aerobic Plate Count testing
