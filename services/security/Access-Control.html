Security Service

Access Control Systems

GiganticFM specializes in providing advanced access control systems that ensure secure and controlled entry for individuals in various premises. Their comprehensive solutions, incorporating biometric readers, card readers, keypads, and proximity devices, offer reliable identification, verification, and access management capabilities. With seamless integration with other security systems, such as video surveillance and alarms, GiganticFM's access control systems provide a centralized and robust security infrastructure. Administrators can easily manage access permissions, define specific areas and time restrictions, and monitor access activities. With their expertise in installation, maintenance, and support, GiganticFM delivers efficient and tailored access control solutions to clients in Abu Dhabi, Dubai, and the UAE, enhancing overall security and operational efficiency.
